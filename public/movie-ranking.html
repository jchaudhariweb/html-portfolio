The Best Movies According to Jaydeep

My top 3 movies of all-time.

AK 47

This is my favourite movie. Inspired by the story of revolutionary innovation of AK47 submachine gun.

The Imitation Game

Really cool World War time movie. This Drama film tells the story of Alan Turing, a British mathematician who helped crack the Enigma code during World War II.

Swades

Super beautiful film. The realisation of love towards homeland is beautifully presented.
